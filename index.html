Methodology & caveats

Holdings snapshot — entity balances are sourced from Arkham Intelligence's Bitcoin Top Holders list (snapshot date 2026-04-22) and are baked directly into this page. No live API calls are made for holdings data.

CEX holdings represent on-chain labeled exchange wallets as identified by Arkham. Coverage is limited to Arkham-labeled addresses; real exchange reserves may be larger.

Institutional / Whale entities include public-company treasuries (Strategy, MARA, etc.), spot ETF custodians (BlackRock IBIT, Fidelity FBTC, etc.), governments, known individual wallets (Satoshi, lost wallets), and other tracked large holders.

"Other On-chain" is computed as circulating supply minus everything tracked above. It absorbs retail holders, miners, unlabeled whale addresses, lost coins, and any entities not in the snapshot.

Circulating supply is fetched live from CoinGecko on page load. If that fails, a fallback constant of 19,800,000 BTC is used.
